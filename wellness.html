Wellness-marketplace

A venue listing and booking platform that helps users discover wellness spaces, compare options, and book sessions easily.

The project focuses on smooth browsing, clear listing details, and a simple booking flow for better customer experience.

1# Venue Discovery Experience

Users can browse wellness venues with clear profiles, service categories, availability insights, and location-based filtering to find the right space faster.

2# Booking and Inquiry Flow

The platform supports a smooth booking journey where users can submit inquiries, choose suitable slots, and complete requests through a streamlined and user-friendly flow.

3# Dynamic Listing Management

Venue data is structured for easy updates, helping admins manage listings, content, and service details without rebuilding pages.

4# Automation and Operations

Airtable and Zapier integrations support backend operations by automating lead capture, routing inquiries, and reducing manual coordination between teams.

5# Scalable No-Code + Custom Setup

Built with Webflow and custom JavaScript, the project combines fast visual development with custom interactions for performance, flexibility, and long-term scalability.
